What is Dead By NightLight?

👻 Dead By NightLight is an asymmetrical horror game where fear is always watching you… 👀

🎮 4 Survivors vs 1 Killer face off across 3 terrifying maps:
🪓 A dark Wood Factory
🧪 A cold National Laboratory
🏛 An underground environment

🛠️ Survivors’ Objective:
Repair 5 computers out of 7
Unlock the exit door
Escape alive 🏃‍♂️💨
Work as a team, stay quiet, and don’t get caught

🔪 Killer’s Objective:
Hunt down all Survivors
Lock them inside freezers 🧊
Keep them trapped until they succumb to the cold 😈

😱 Hide, run, repair, or hunt — every match is different!

✨ Play now and choose your destiny! ✨
