What is Dead By NightLight?

👻 Dead By NightLight is an asymmetrical horror game where fear is always watching you… 👀 🎮 4 Survivors vs 1 Killer face off across 3 terrifying maps: 🪓 a dark Wood Factory 🧪 a cold National Laboratory 🏛 or the background of an underground map. 🛠️ Survivors’ Objective: Repair 5 computers out of 7 to unlock an exit door and escape alive 🏃‍♂️💨 Work as a team, stay quiet, and don’t get caught! 🔪 Killer’s Objective: Hunt down all Survivors and lock them inside freezers 🧊 Keep them trapped until they succumb to the cold… escaping won’t be easy 😈 😱 Hide, run, repair, or hunt — every match is different! Do you have what it takes to survive the night… or rule the terror? ✨ Play now and choose your destiny! ✨
